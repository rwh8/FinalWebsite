    Ice Shapes

Different Ice Shapes For Different Drinks

Sphere

Ice in the shape of a medium sized sphere. Using this shape in your glass helps to spread the surface area of the ice, leading to a slower melt, with an elegant look.



Ice Block

A larger than average Ice Cube, known better as a block. Best served with a Whiskey or Scotch for a longer lasting drink, with less water dilution.



Crushed Ice

Crushed ice is a necessity when mixing drinks. It helps to dilute spirit-forward cocktails for a less overpowering flavor, while creating a refreshingly chilled drink.
