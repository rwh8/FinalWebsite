Glasses For All Tastes

Lowball

The Lowball glass is one typically used to serve Scotches and Whiskeys, or a classic drink such as an Old Fashioned.



Collins

A taller glass, the Collins a perfect pick for most cocktails. It can be dressed up or dressed down with the appropriate garnish.



Martini

The classic and well-known Martini glass is a timeless piece, and a necessity to any collection. Make it dry with a nice lemon peel garnish and it will immediately become a fan favorite, just make sure it's shaken, not stirred.
